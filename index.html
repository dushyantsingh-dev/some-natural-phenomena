What are charges ?

Everything around us is made up of atoms. Atoms have three fundamental particles protons, neutrons and electrons. Protons are positively charged, neutrons are not charged and electrons are negatively charged. When an atom gains or loses electons it gets a charge.

How does lightning occur ?

During thunderstorm, when a charged cloud passes over an uncharged cloud, uncharged cloud acquires opposite charge by induction and the two clouds attract. When they combine with each other, they produce a large amount of heat, light and sound also known as lightning.

What causes earthquakes ?

The crust of Earth is divided into large pieces called tectonic plates. When the tectonic plates slide over one another they cause disturbances which vibrations that spread in all directions. This is called an earthquake
